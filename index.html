Clothing Size Converters

International size converters can be useful for seeing what size you wear in another country, however it is important to remember size conversions are only approximates. Every clothing manufacturer and brand uses their own variations of “standard” sizing. While countries often have standards boards that suggest measurement ranges and size nomenclatures, apparel companies are not required to follow these recommendations . This leads to great variation in size definitions among brands, regardless of the country in which the clothing is made.
